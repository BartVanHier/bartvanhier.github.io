Monochrome Animals

Monochrome means black and white.

Here are several colours a monochrome animal can't be:

  • Brown
  • Orange
  • Green
  • Seafoam

This is what an animal needs to be a monochrome animal:

  1. Be an animal.
  2. Be partially black (optional).
  3. Be partially white (optional).
  4. Be partially grey (optional).
  5. Not be a colour beside those mentioned above.
